PURPOSE

Welcome to Boba Bae!

  • Use "WAD" or the Arrow keys to play as Kevin Nguyen. Move around the map and collect different items to appease your "hangry" girlfriend, Vivian Tran!
  • Collect as many items as you can before the timer strikes zero
  • Start the game to play music! Toggle on/off at the bottom of the screen.
  • DANGER: watch out for special items that can decrease your health. Be careful when jumping!

Game Over!

  • Great job, Kevin!
  • Vivian is satisfied and you live to see another day
