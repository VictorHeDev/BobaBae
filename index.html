PURPOSE
Did you forget how to play?

Use "WAD" or the Arrow keys in order to catch items falling from the sky! Compete with yourself or others to beat the high score before the timer runs out. Toggle the music/sound effects on the top right of the game after you press start!

Welcome to Boba Bae!

  • Use "WAD" or the Arrow keys to play as Kevin Nguyen. Move around the map and collect different items to appease your "hangry" girlfriend, Vivian Tran!
  • Collect as many items as you can before the timer strikes zero
  • Start the game to play music! Toggle on/off on the top right of the screen.
  • DANGER: watch out for special items that can decrease your health. Be careful when jumping!
